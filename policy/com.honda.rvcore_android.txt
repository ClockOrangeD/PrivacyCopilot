What personal information do we collect from you?
Personal information refers to various information recorded electronically or by other means that is related to identified or identifiable natural persons, excluding anonymized information. The personal information mentioned in this privacy policy includes: your name, title, mobile number, personal image, mobile device information, service record information.

Personal sensitive information refers to personal information that, once leaked, unlawfully provided or abused, may endanger personal and property safety, easily cause damage to personal reputation, physical and mental health, or discriminatory treatment. The personal sensitive information collected in this privacy policy includes geographical location information and images.

We default not to collect personal sensitive information. If it is necessary to collect, we will obtain your separate authorized consent at the time of collection, and you can independently set the consent period by contacting us through the customer service phone number provided in the mobile app or by email.

When you register our "Connected Car Platform" / "Smart Guide Interconnected APP" / "Smart Guide Cloud Key" account, we will collect, store, and use the following personal information: including but not limited to your name, title, mobile number, language. The above information provided by you will be obtained once when you submit the registration and will continue to authorize our use during your use of this service. We will keep your information until you cancel your account. When you cancel your account, we will stop using and delete the above information.

When you use our "Connected Car Platform" / "Smart Guide Interconnected APP" / "Smart Guide Cloud Key" products or services, we will collect, store, and use the following personal information: including but not limited to your name, title, mobile number.
In order to ensure the safe operation of software products and services, and to provide you with a secure and trustworthy product and user environment, we may collect your device information, including device model, unique device identifier (such as IMEI/Android ID/IDFA/OPENUDID/GUID/IMSI information), device MAC address, operating system type and model, screen resolution, telecom operator, software installation list, software version number, system language. The application collects your device MAC address information, installed application list in silent/background state, for the purpose of network requests and identification of malicious applications. To prevent malicious programs and ensure safe operation, we collect information about installed applications or running processes, overall application operation and installation usage, usage and frequency, application crash information, application source, performance data. In order to ensure timely response when using the Bluetooth key, we will regularly start this app to ensure communication between the Bluetooth key and the vehicle.

Purpose: Use the Samsung watch to control the vehicle through voice commands.

Content collected: Detection of running programs to ensure normal communication between the Samsung watch and the phone app.
