What personal information do we collect from you
Personal information refers to various information recorded by electronic or other means that is related to identified or identifiable natural persons, excluding anonymized information. The personal information mentioned in this Privacy Policy includes: your name, title, mobile phone number, electronic invoice billing information, age, date of birth, gender, personal image, identity card information, passport information, permit for residents of Hong Kong and Macau, permit for residents of Taiwan, driving license information, vehicle purchase invoice information, transaction and consumption records, browsing history, mobile device information, geographical location information, voice, calendar information.

Personal sensitive information refers to personal information that, once leaked, illegally provided, or abused, may endanger personal and property safety, cause harm to personal reputation and physical and mental health, or discriminatory treatment, etc. The personal sensitive information collected in this Privacy Policy includes: property information (such as transaction and consumption records), identity card information, passport information, permit for residents of Hong Kong and Macau, permit for residents of Taiwan, driving license information, geographical location information, browsing history, heart rate information.

By default, we do not collect personal sensitive information. If it is necessary to collect, we will obtain your separate authorization consent at the time of collection, and you can set the authorization period independently through the customer service phone number reserved on the mobile APP.

When you register an account in the Zhidao Internet APP, Haina Xinsi will collect, store, and share your personal information, including your name, title, mobile phone number, identity card information, passport information, driving license information, permit for residents of Hong Kong and Macau, permit for residents of Taiwan, vehicle purchase invoice information, in conjunction with the vehicle manufacturer and vehicle seller. The above-mentioned information you provide will be obtained once when you register and will continue to be authorized for use by us during your use of this Service. We will keep your information until you cancel your account. When you cancel your account, we will stop using and delete the above information.

When you use the Zhidao Internet APP products or services, Haina Xinsi will collect, store, and share your personal information in conjunction with the vehicle manufacturer and vehicle seller, including your name, title, mobile phone number, age, date of birth, gender, personal image, voice, transaction and consumption records, browsing history, geographical location information, calendar information, and electronic invoice billing information.

When you use products or services such as driving behavior reports and travel services, Haina Xinsi will collect, store, and share your personal information in conjunction with the vehicle manufacturer and vehicle seller, including geographical location information, voice, heart rate information, browsing history, Wi-Fi list, currently connected Wi-Fi information, device sensor information, and screenshots.

In order to ensure the safe operation of software products and services, and provide you with a safe and reliable product and user environment, Haina Xinsi may collect your device information, including device model, unique device identifier (such as IMEI / Android ID / IDFA / OPENUDID / GUID / IMSI information), device serial number, device MAC address, operating system type and model, screen resolution, telecommunications operator, SIM card status, SIM card serial number, SIM card country code, phone type, installed software list, software version number, system language, device model, screen size, network type, current running processes, device screen width, device screen height, device screen pixels, network identity information (IP address). In order to prevent malicious programs and ensure secure operation, we collect information about installed applications or running processes, overall operation and installation usage, usage and frequency, application crash information, application source, and performance data. To ensure a timely response when using the Bluetooth key, we will regularly start this APP to ensure communication between the Bluetooth key and the vehicle.
